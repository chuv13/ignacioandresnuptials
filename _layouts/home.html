Our Story

A serendipitous meeting

It all started when we met at the Rogaciano M. Mercado Memorial Hospital. After a while, she left for the UAE and we embarked on a long-distance relationship that lasted for two emotional years. Despite the physical separation, we both shared the dream of becoming NZRNs. Finally, after enduring the trials of being apart, we were reunited in Australia.

Fast forward to today, and here we are, still going strong after all the struggles and hardships we've faced together. We continue to support each other, making our love story a testament to our enduring bond.
